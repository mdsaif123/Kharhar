Primary School Kharhar

In my village, there is a primary school which is the main source of education for the children. This school is situated in a small building with one "headmistress" and eight teachers. The students are provided with meals, and the school is closed on every Fridays. This school plays a crucial role in the development of our village and provides quality education to the children here.
